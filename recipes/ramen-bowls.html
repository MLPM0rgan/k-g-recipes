Ramen Bowls

Description

While it may take a bit of effort, these scrumptious ramen bowls are sure to be a crowd pleaser. If you're looking to come home from a long day at work and spend way too long in the kitchen, then you've come to the right place!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 tbps unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp seasame oil
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 4 cups ckicken stock
  • 2 large eggs
  • 0.5 cups scallions, sliced
  • 2 packs ramen noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  2. Apply oil, salt, and pepper to chicken breasts
  3. Melt butter in oven-safe skillet on medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes. Flip, and cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  4. Transfer skillet to oven and roast for 15-20 minutes, until chicken is fully cooked (internal temp of 165)
  5. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat, until shimmering. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for a few minutes until softened. Add the soy sauce and mirin, and stir to combine. Cook for another minute.
  6. Add the stock, cover, and bring to a boil. Remove the lid, and let simmer uncovered for 15 minutes, and season with salt, to taste.
  7. Fill a pot with enough water to cover the eggs, and bring to a boil. Gently lower the eggs (still cold from the drive) into the boiling water, and let simmer for 7 minutes (for a slightly-runny yolk) to 8 minutes (for a soft, set up yolk).
  8. Meanwhile, fill a large bowl with ice water. When the timer finishes, transfer the eggs to the ice bath to stop the cooking process. Wait at least 5 minutes, or until cool enough to handle, then carefully peel away the shell and slice in half, lengthwise. Set aside until ready to serve.
  9. Chop the scallions and chicken into thin pieces. Set aside. When the eggs finish cooking, add the ramen noodles to the boiling water.
  10. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until soft, then divide the noodles into two large bowls. Add the sliced chicken and the ramen broth. Top with fresh scallions and the soft boiled egg. Serve immediately.
