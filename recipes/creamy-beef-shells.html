Creamy Beef & Shells

Description

It may not be fancy, but man will it hit the SPOT! This weeknight classic gets a slightly spruced-up treatment that is sure to have the kids begging for seconds (and the wife too!)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz medium pasta shells
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 0.5 medium sweet onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 tsp italian seasoning
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • “Several dashes” Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 0.75 cup heavy cream
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 6 oz shredded extra-sharp cheddar cheese
